My Final Project!

My final project is a simple game called River Rush.
In the game, you play as a fish swimming against a current who must eat smaller fish to grow bigger.
However, if you touch a larger fish, you will be killed!
There are also coins to collect and cosmetics you can purchase from the shop with them!
You can try the game link posted below. Remember: your player is controlled by the mouse.
Try to aim for a new high score!
